What is this? Who Are You?

Welcome, friends and wannabe-adventureres, to the resting place of the (in)famous Might Nein. Known throughout (some) of Exandria for our daring and spectacular and totally, definitely well-thought-out exploits, we are the best mercenary group for higher this side of the Shearing Channel. We boast a wide range of skillsets, including two clerics (capable of healing wonders), a warlock and a wizard for all your magicking needs, rogues for every occasion, a cobalt monk, and (sometimes) even a barbarian.
